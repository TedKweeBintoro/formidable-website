Wage & Hour Litigation

Our wage and hour experts provide comprehensive analysis of employment data and legal compliance in complex labor litigation. We analyze payroll records, employment contracts, and labor laws to identify compliance patterns and support legal proceedings with data-driven insights.

Expert Analysis for Employment Litigation

We combine economic analysis with deep understanding of labor laws to provide clear, defensible assessments in wage and hour disputes

Payroll Analysis

Comprehensive examination of payroll records, time sheets, and compensation data. We identify patterns of underpayment, overtime violations, and off-the-clock work using advanced data analytics and statistical methods.

Labor Law Compliance

Detailed assessment of compliance with federal, state, and local labor laws including FLSA, state wage and hour laws, and industry-specific regulations. We evaluate policies, practices, and their implementation.

Overtime Calculation Disputes

Expert analysis of overtime calculations, regular rate determinations, and compensable time. We examine work schedules, bonus structures, and compensation methods to identify and quantify violations.

Employment Classification Issues

Assessment of employee versus independent contractor classifications, exempt versus non-exempt determinations, and joint employer relationships. We apply economic reality tests and legal standards to classification disputes.

Class Action Support

Statistical analysis for class certification, damages calculations for class-wide relief, and assessment of commonality and typicality. We provide robust analyses that withstand Daubert challenges.

Damages Quantification

Precise calculation of back wages, liquidated damages, and penalties. We develop damages models that account for interest, tax implications, and mitigation factors in wage and hour cases.
