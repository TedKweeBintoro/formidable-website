Radiology

Our radiology experts provide medical imaging expertise for litigation support and diagnostic evaluation. We deliver expert testimony on imaging interpretation, radiation exposure cases, and diagnostic accuracy in medical malpractice matters with the highest standards of radiological expertise.

Expert Radiological Analysis for Complex Litigation

Our board-certified radiologists provide comprehensive imaging analysis and expert testimony for medical-legal cases

Medical Imaging Analysis

Expert interpretation of all imaging modalities including X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, and nuclear medicine studies. We provide detailed analysis of imaging findings and their clinical significance in litigation contexts.

Radiation Exposure Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of radiation exposure incidents, including medical radiation overdose, occupational exposure, and environmental radiation cases. We assess dose calculations, biological effects, and long-term health risks.

Diagnostic Accuracy Review

Critical assessment of diagnostic imaging interpretations, including evaluation of missed diagnoses, interpretation errors, and delays in diagnosis. We determine whether imaging studies met the standard of care.

Imaging Standard of Care Evaluation

Expert review of whether appropriate imaging studies were ordered, performed correctly, and interpreted according to accepted radiological standards. We assess protocol adherence and quality control measures.

Interventional Radiology Assessment

Evaluation of complications and outcomes from interventional radiological procedures including biopsies, drainage procedures, and vascular interventions. We assess technique, complications, and standard of care.

Trauma Imaging Analysis

Expert interpretation of imaging studies in trauma cases, including motor vehicle accidents, falls, and assault cases. We correlate imaging findings with injury mechanisms and clinical outcomes.
