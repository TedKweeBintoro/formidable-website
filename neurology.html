Our Methodology

Clear, defensible steps designed for complex neurologic matters and courtroom scrutiny.

1

Intake & Record Synthesis

Review encounter timelines, imaging, EEG, medication history, and clinical notes to frame key neurologic questions.

2

Diagnostic Correlation

Align clinical findings with neuroimaging, electrophysiology, labs, and exam data to establish causation and severity.

3

Evidence & Differential Review

Apply guideline-based standards, peer-reviewed literature, and alternative explanations to stress-test conclusions.

4

Reporting & Testimony Support

Deliver clear opinions, demonstratives, and deposition preparation tailored to judge and jury comprehension.
