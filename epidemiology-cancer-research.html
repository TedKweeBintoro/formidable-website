Expert Medical Analysis for Complex Litigation

Our epidemiology and cancer research team provides evidence-based assessments of disease causation, exposure risks, and medical outcomes

Cancer Causality Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of potential causal relationships between exposures and cancer development. We analyze epidemiological data, individual exposure histories, and scientific literature to assess causation in cases involving various cancer types.

Exposure Risk Assessment

Detailed analysis of exposure pathways, dose-response relationships, and risk factors. Our experts quantify exposure levels and evaluate their potential health impacts using established epidemiological methods and models.

Epidemiological Studies

Design and evaluation of epidemiological studies to investigate disease patterns, risk factors, and causal associations. We provide critical review of existing studies and develop new research when needed for litigation support.

Medical Literature Review

Systematic review and synthesis of relevant medical and scientific literature. We evaluate the strength of evidence, identify knowledge gaps, and provide comprehensive summaries of current scientific understanding.

Occupational Disease Assessment

Expert analysis of work-related disease claims, including asbestos-related diseases, occupational cancers, and chemical exposures. We evaluate workplace exposure histories and their relationship to disease development.

Product Liability Health Assessment

Evaluation of health claims related to consumer products, pharmaceuticals, and medical devices. We assess the scientific evidence for alleged health effects and provide objective analysis of causation.
