Expert Toxicological Analysis for Litigation

Our board-certified toxicologists provide scientific assessment of chemical exposures, poisonings, and substance-related health effects

Chemical Exposure Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of acute and chronic exposures across occupational, environmental, and consumer settings. Includes dose estimation, exposure route analysis, and biological effect interpretation.

Poison Identification and Effects

Toxicological assessment of poisoning cases, including substance identification, route of exposure, symptomology, and causation analysis.

Environmental Toxin Assessment

Evaluation of environmental contamination involving air, water, or soil pollutants. Includes risk quantification and community exposure assessment.

Dose-Response Relationships

Application of pharmacokinetics and toxicokinetics to determine thresholds, toxic doses, and safe exposure limits in both individual and population-level cases.

Occupational Toxicology

Analysis of workplace exposures including metals, solvents, and industrial chemicals. Includes regulatory compliance evaluation and causality assessments.

Product Safety Evaluation

Investigation of adverse health effects linked to pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and consumer products. Includes ingredient safety, contamination assessment, and regulatory interpretation.
