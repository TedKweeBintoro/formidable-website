Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology experts provide comprehensive analysis in vision-related medical litigation and eye injury cases. We deliver expert assessments of ocular trauma, visual impairment, and eye-related medical malpractice claims with the highest standards of medical expertise.

Expert Ophthalmologic Analysis for Litigation

Our board-certified ophthalmologists provide detailed medical assessments and expert testimony in complex eye-related litigation matters

Ocular Trauma Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of eye injuries resulting from accidents, assaults, or workplace incidents. We assess the mechanism of injury, extent of damage, and long-term visual consequences using advanced diagnostic techniques.

Visual Impairment Evaluation

Detailed assessment of visual function loss, including visual acuity, visual field defects, and functional vision impairment. We quantify the impact on daily activities and occupational capabilities.

Retinal Damage Analysis

Expert evaluation of retinal injuries and diseases, including macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and retinal detachment. We assess causation, progression, and treatment adequacy in medical litigation.

Eye Injury Causation Studies

Scientific analysis of the relationship between alleged incidents and resulting eye injuries. We evaluate medical records, imaging studies, and apply ophthalmologic principles to determine causation.

Medical Malpractice Review

Assessment of standard of care in ophthalmologic treatment, including surgical complications, misdiagnosis, and delayed treatment. We evaluate whether care met accepted medical standards.
