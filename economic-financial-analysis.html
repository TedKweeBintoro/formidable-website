Comprehensive Securities Litigation Support

Our securities and financial analysis team combines academic excellence with real-world expertise to provide clear, defensible analyses in high-stakes litigation

Securities Class Actions

Our experts provide comprehensive support in securities class action litigation, including event studies, damages calculations, and loss causation analysis. We have experience with cases involving alleged violations of Section 10(b) and Rule 10b-5.

Financial Disclosure Analysis

We analyze the adequacy and accuracy of financial disclosures, including assessments of materiality, evaluation of accounting treatments, and quantification of the impact of alleged misstatements or omissions on stock prices.

Insider Trading Investigations

Our team conducts detailed analyses of trading patterns, information flows, and market movements to support or defend against insider trading allegations. We use advanced statistical methods to identify suspicious trading activity.

Valuation Disputes

We provide independent valuation analyses for disputes involving mergers and acquisitions, appraisal rights, fairness opinions, and solvency determinations. Our valuations are grounded in financial theory and market evidence.
