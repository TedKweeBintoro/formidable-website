Bankruptcy & Restructuring

Our bankruptcy and restructuring experts provide critical analyses in high-profile bankruptcy proceedings using advanced methodologies to assess financial health and viability. We design and execute large-scale empirical analyses for complex restructuring matters, delivering clarity in the most challenging financial situations.

Expert Analysis for Complex Restructuring

We bring deep expertise in financial distress, reorganization, and bankruptcy proceedings to support legal teams and stakeholders in achieving optimal outcomes

Financial Health Assessment

Comprehensive analysis of company financial condition, including solvency evaluations, liquidity assessments, and cash flow projections. We identify key financial stress indicators and provide detailed assessments of going concern viability.

Asset Valuation Models

Sophisticated valuation analyses for distressed assets, business units, and entire enterprises. Our models incorporate market conditions, industry dynamics, and company-specific factors to provide robust valuations for bankruptcy proceedings.

DLOM and Price Impact Modeling

Expert analysis of discounts for lack of marketability (DLOM) and price impact in distressed situations. We quantify the effects of illiquidity and market conditions on asset values using advanced econometric techniques.

Venture Investment Analysis

Specialized analysis of venture capital and private equity investments in bankruptcy contexts. We evaluate portfolio company valuations, waterfall analyses, and liquidity preference implications in restructuring scenarios.

Fraudulent Transfer Analysis

Rigorous examination of potentially fraudulent transfers and preferences. We analyze transaction timing, consideration adequacy, and solvency at the time of transfer to support or defend against avoidance actions.

Plan Feasibility Studies

Detailed assessment of proposed reorganization plans, including financial projections, capital structure analyses, and sensitivity testing. We evaluate the likelihood of successful emergence from bankruptcy.
