Expert Toxicological Analysis for Litigation

Our board-certified toxicologists provide scientific assessment of chemical exposures, poisonings, and substance-related health effects

Chemical Exposure Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of acute and chronic chemical exposures in occupational, environmental, and consumer settings. We assess exposure pathways, doses, and biological effects using established toxicological principles.

Poison Identification and Effects

Expert analysis of poisoning cases including identification of toxic substances, assessment of exposure routes, and evaluation of clinical manifestations. We provide detailed toxicological profiles and causation assessments.

Environmental Toxin Assessment

Evaluation of environmental contamination cases involving soil, water, and air pollutants. We assess community exposures, calculate risk levels, and determine health impacts from environmental toxins.

Dose-Response Relationships

Scientific analysis of dose-response relationships to determine threshold levels, toxic doses, and safe exposure limits. We apply pharmacokinetic and toxicokinetic principles to assess substance effects.

Occupational Toxicology

Assessment of workplace chemical exposures, including industrial solvents, metals, and hazardous substances. We evaluate compliance with occupational exposure limits and determine work-related health effects.

Product Safety Evaluation

Toxicological assessment of consumer products, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. We evaluate ingredient safety, contamination issues, and adverse reactions to determine product-related health risks.

Our Toxicological Methodology
